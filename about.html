About

balancebook by of deep origin LLC

balancebook is a personal finance tracker for iPhone, built for clarity, privacy, and daily use. Track your money by hand or auto-import transactions from your bank, set budgets that fit your life, and review your month at a glance — without spreadsheets, fiddly settings, or noise.

It's designed around the way people actually manage their money. Fast manual entry for the expenses you remember in the moment. Recurring entries for the things you'd rather not have to think about each month. Account syncing for visibility into where you stand. Shared ledgers for partners and households who track money together. Category trends and budget history so you can spot the places where things are drifting before they become a problem.

Keep your personal, household, and side-business finances in separate ledgers when you need that separation. Export your data anytime — it's yours. Stay private by default; there are no ads, no trackers, and we don't sell or share what you log.
