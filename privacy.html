Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

balancebook is built with your privacy as a priority. This policy explains what data we collect, how it's used, and how it's stored.

Data We Collect

balancebook does not collect any personal data. All financial data you enter — entries, categories, budgets, ledgers — is stored locally on your device and, if you enable iCloud sync, in your personal iCloud account.

iCloud Sync

If you use the Pro plan, your data is synced via Apple's CloudKit to your private iCloud account. This data is only accessible to you and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We have no access to your iCloud data.

Analytics & Tracking

balancebook does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not track your behavior inside the app.

In-App Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how purchase data is handled.

Data Export & Import

You can export your data at any time as a JSON or CSV file. This file is generated locally on your device and shared only when you explicitly choose to share it. We have no access to exported files.

Children's Privacy

balancebook is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
