Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

balancebook is built with your privacy in mind. This policy explains what data we collect, how it's stored, and how it's used.

Account & Authentication

balancebook uses Google Cloud to manage accounts. You can sign in with Apple ID, which shares only a private relay email address with us — your real email is never disclosed unless you choose to share it. You may also use the app without signing in; an anonymous account is created automatically on your device.

We store a minimal user profile (user ID, display name, and relay email) in Google Cloud to support features like shared ledgers. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Financial Data

All data you enter — entries, categories, budgets, ledgers, tags, and recurring templates — is stored securely in Google Cloud, hosted in the United States. Your data is associated with your account and is not shared with or sold to third parties.

Your financial data is stored on Google's servers. Access to stored data is restricted to authorized balancebook personnel and is only used when necessary to provide technical support or investigate a reported issue — never for any other purpose. We do not sell or share your personal financial data with third parties.

Bank Sync (Pro)

If you use the Auto or Hybrid entry modes, balancebook connects to your bank accounts via Plaid, a third-party financial data aggregator. When you link a bank account:

  • You authenticate directly with your bank through the aggregator's secure interface — we never see your banking credentials.
  • The aggregator provides us with a read-only access token to retrieve your transactions. We can only read transaction data — we cannot move money, make payments, or modify your accounts in any way. This token is encrypted before being stored and is only decrypted server-side when syncing transactions.
  • Transaction data (amounts, dates, merchant names) is stored in your account under the same terms as manually entered data.
  • You can disconnect your bank at any time from Settings, which permanently deletes the access token from our servers.

Plaid acts as a data sub-processor under a written data processing agreement and handles your data solely for the purpose of retrieving your transactions. See Plaid's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your data.

Analytics & Tracking

balancebook does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not track your behavior inside the app.

In-App Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — you can update or correct your account information at any time from Settings.
  • Deletion — you can permanently delete your account and all associated data from Settings.
  • Portability — you can export all your financial data at any time as a JSON or CSV file directly from the app.

To exercise any of these rights, use the controls in the app or contact us.

Data Export & Deletion

You can export all your data at any time as a JSON or CSV file directly from the app. You can also delete your account from Settings, which permanently deletes all your data from our servers — including ledgers, entries, categories, budgets, and any connected bank accounts. We do not retain your data after account deletion.

Data Security

Your data is transmitted to and from our servers using encrypted connections (TLS). Bank access tokens are encrypted at rest using industry-standard key management before being stored. Google Cloud infrastructure includes firewall protection and physical security measures.

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no system can guarantee 100% security. If you have concerns about the security of your data, please contact us.

Children's Privacy

balancebook is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
